Welcome to the Scavenger Hunt of Sculptures

For this activity you will get into 2 groups, group 1 and group 2. Once you have figured out your groups, click on your respective number to start the activity. Here are the rules:

  1. Pick a start spot on campus - ex: Outside the Environmental Building
  2. Read the riddles carefully and hurry to the sculpture you think its about.
  3. Find the plaque and enter in the artist's name WITH ALL LOWERCASE!
  4. Once you get it right, move on to the others
  5. If you need to use a hint, click on the hint button and the hint will drop down. But try not to ;)
  6. The first group back to the starting location wins!
