Riddle One

In a place where knowledge blooms and thoughts take wing
I rise from the ground, a curious thing.
With steps leading up, towards the sky's embrace
Find me where art and ambition interlace.
What am I, a wonder, both bold and serene,
A journey upward, in a vibrant scene?

Riddle Two

Amidst the rocks where knowledge grows,
I watch the scene, as life ebbs and flows.
Figures in motion, some lifting with care,
A dance of labor, a whimsical air.
Rest on the ledge, where strength meets the mind,
What am I, a tribute to those intertwined?
