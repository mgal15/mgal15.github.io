MLG

Project Proposal

Goal

Engage students in a cross campus scavenger hunt to find interesting places/things

Ideas

  1. Add multiple levels of hints, each decrement total points given for each question
  2. Add a server so students could add a group name or their name and it saves their total points and keeps a record of who played and when
    • Express
    • Mongodb
  3. Add more spots and push it beyond just sculptures by thinking of fun/cool places on campus that students should check out - making it fun but also helpful to learn the area.

Purpose

I want the purpose of this project to be for new students. This could be something posted about or on bulletin boards, and when a student sees it, they can play the game whenever. Its to help students get an idea for what is around campus.
