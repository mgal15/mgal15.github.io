How to Internet: A guided Tour through the past

By Jenny Odell

At my job teaching college students, I'm occasionally mistaken for one. I've found that a good way to make students take me seriously on the first day is mention that I remember a time before the internet. Of course that's not true given a precise interpretation of what the internet actually is. But I'm certainly old enough to remember PSA-like videos coaxing the casual newbie into the exciting, if intimidating, world of cyberspace.
